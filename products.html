Products

We sell many different types and brands of seasoning that would be perfect for a cookout or for the family dinner. We carry brands such as Goya, El Mexicano, Knorr to name a few.

We offer premium grade Mexican-styled cut meats such as fajitas, chicken, and pork. We also carry marinated and prepared chicken and fajitas ready to be cooked.

South American and Carribean frozen products are also offered. Products such as yuca, malanga, tostones, and tapas. Fresh plantains both green and ripenned are also sold.

We sell a variety of domestic and Mexican beer such as Victoria, Corona, Modelo, and Budlight

During the summer season, we sell freshly cut fruit cups! Choose from mango, watermelon, melon, cucumber, and jicama. You could also try our "Mangonadas", mango flavored ice cream with chamoy sauce and frozen pieces of mango.



Come in to discover all that we have to offer!
